The first public release of Pikos for macOS and Linux. Notes, tasks, and calendar in one app, stored on your device.

**Editor.** Rich text with headings, lists, task checklists, code blocks, tables, and images. Slash menu to insert any block, floating format toolbar, find in page, autosave. Paste Markdown and it converts.

**Pages.** Quick Add reads a single line of plain English: "Call dentist tomorrow 9am high priority #health" sets the date, time, priority, and tag. Status, four priority levels, tags, folders with custom colors, smart views for Today and Inbox.

**Calendar.** Week view next to your editor. Drag to create, drag to reschedule, drag edges to resize. Recurring schedules with sensible handling of missed days. Reminders fire as native notifications.

**Search.** Full-text search over titles and content, ranked results with previews.

**Import and export.** Bring an Obsidian vault or CSV exports from TickTick and Todoist. Export as a SQLite backup, a Markdown folder, or CSV. Imports are reversible.

**Your device, your data.** No account, no tracking, nothing leaves your machine. The only network request is the update check, and you can turn it off in Settings.
